# <a name="offboarding-machines-from-the-microsoft-defender-atp-service"></a>Offboarding machines from the Microsoft Defenderi ATP service

Kui vajate abi seadme väljalülitamisel Defenderi ATP-teenusest, lugege oma opsüsteemi juhiseid.  

- Lisateavet Windows 10 kohta leiate teemast Seadmest väljas [asuvad seadmed Microsoft Defenderi ATP teenusest.](/windows/security/threat-protection/microsoft-defender-atp/offboard-machines#offboard-windows-10-devices)
- Mitte-Windows seadmete kohta leiate lisateavet teemast Seadmest väljas [Windows seadmed.](/windows/security/threat-protection/microsoft-defender-atp/configure-endpoints-non-windows#offboard-non-windows-devices)
