- Veenduge, et teie tellimus kehtib **arve** \> **tellimuste** lehel [Microsoft 365 halduskeskus](https://portal.office.com/adminportal/home#/subscriptions).

- Otsige varasemate laekumata **arvete** teated \> **arvete** lehel [Microsoft 365 halduskeskus](https://portal.office.com/adminportal/home#/billoverview).
