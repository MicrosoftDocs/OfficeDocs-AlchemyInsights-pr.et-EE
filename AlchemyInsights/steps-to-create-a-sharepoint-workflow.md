# <a name="steps-to-create-a-sharepoint-workflow"></a>SharePointi töövoo loomise juhised

- Kui te ei saa SharePointi 2010 kinnitamise töövoogu lisada, lugege teemat [vaikimisi 2010 kinnitamise töövoo lisamine](https://docs.microsoft.com/alchemyinsights/can-t-add-default-2010-approval-workflow).
- Töövoogude loomine.
    - [2013 töövoog SharePoint Designerist](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/dev/general-development/creating-a-workflow-by-using-sharepoint-designer-and-the-sharepoint-wo)
    - [2010 töövoog SharePoint Designerist](https://support.office.com/article/introduction-to-designing-and-customizing-workflows-32c9c0bf-5e20-4f74-8b9c-d3ea79f2962b)
    - [SharePointi töövoo tutvustus](https://support.office.com/article/introduction-to-sharepoint-workflow-07982276-54e8-4e17-8699-5056eff4d9e3)

- Töövoo tõrkeotsing ja silumine:
    - Silumise töövood sarnanevad eelmiste versioonidega.  Lisateavet leiate teemast [SharePointi töövoogude silumiseks](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/dev/general-development/debugging-sharepoint-server-workflows).
    - Tõrkeotsingu kohta leiate lisateavet teemast [SharePoint Serveri töövoo valideerimise tõrgete tõrkeotsing](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/dev/general-development/troubleshooting-sharepoint-server-workflow-validation-errors-in-visio).
