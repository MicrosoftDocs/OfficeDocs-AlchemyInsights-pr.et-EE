**Töövoogude tõrkeotsing ja silumiseks SharePointis**

Lugege teemat [SharePointi töövoo valideerimise tõrgete tõrkeotsing](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/dev/general-development/troubleshooting-sharepoint-server-workflow-validation-errors-in-visio) ja [SharePointi töövoogude silumiseks](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/dev/general-development/debugging-sharepoint-server-workflows).
