# <a name="classic-sharepoint-audit-log-reports"></a>Klassikaline SharePointi auditi aruanded

- Logi **sisu vaadata** auditiaruanne ei toetata SharePoint Online. Kui proovite salvestada aruanne saadetakse viga: **kirjeteta**.

- Teil on vaja kasutada otsingufunktsiooni auditi logifaili turvalisuse & vastavuse Center allalaadimiseks SharePointi ja OneDrive seotud auditi palke. Lisateabe saamiseks vaadake jaotist [Otsing auditilogisse](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/securitycompliance/search-the-audit-log-in-security-and-compliance#search-the-audit-log).

- SharePointis või OneDrive toetavad vaikimisi auditeeritud järgmist: [faili-ja lehe](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/securitycompliance/search-the-audit-log-in-security-and-compliance#file-and-page-activities), [kausta](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/securitycompliance/search-the-audit-log-in-security-and-compliance#folder-activities), [ühiskasutus ja juurdepääsu taotleda tegevuse](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/securitycompliance/search-the-audit-log-in-security-and-compliance#sharing-and-access-request-activities), sünkroonimine, ning [tegevus](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/securitycompliance/search-the-audit-log-in-security-and-compliance#synchronization-activities) [saidi halduse tegevuse](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/securitycompliance/search-the-audit-log-in-security-and-compliance#site-administration-activities).
